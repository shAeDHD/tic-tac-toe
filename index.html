Game Rules

On each turn, the player with priority will click one of the squares in the grid above.
Once a square is clicked, priority passes to the next player.

A player wins when they have three of their symbols in
a continous row, be that by horizontal, vertical, or diagonal means.

When ready, select a Game Mode from the options below.
